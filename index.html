About The Event

R10CSSS is a summer school organized by ANITS IEEE Computer society. This program is aimed to bring students especially across the globe on a common platform to hone their skills in the area of Sensors, Networks & IOT Applications by attending hands-on sessions and solving real time problems. Many eminent researchers and experts from Industries from different countries are ready to share their valuable experience to the students.

F.A.Q

  • What is the focus of R10CSSS?

    R10CSSS aims to expose participants to leading-edge technologies in IoT (Internet of Things), Computing, and Networks. It will also address industry requirements and prepare final-year students for employment after graduation.

  • Who can participate in R10CSSS?

    Third or fourth-year undergraduate students are invited to participate in the R10CSSS.

  • What does the registration fee cover?

    This fee covers accommodation, food, and lecture notes for all participants during the four-day event.

  • Will participants receive a certificate after completing the Summer School?

    Yes, all participants who successfully complete the four-day Summer School will receive a Certificate of Participation.
